Understanding the CBSE Curriculum Structure

The CBSE curriculum is designed to promote academic excellence, critical thinking, creativity, and holistic growth from the foundational years to senior secondary education.

At Arulmigu Meenakshi Amman Public School, the CBSE curriculum is implemented with a student-centric and activity-based teaching approach.

  • Strong fundamentals in Mathematics, Science, Languages, and Social Studies
  • Skill-based learning aligned with NEP 2020
  • Continuous assessment and competency-based evaluations
  • Digital learning integration and experiential education
  • Focus on communication, problem-solving, and logical reasoning

By adopting innovative teaching methodologies, AMAPS ensures students are future-ready.
